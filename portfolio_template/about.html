About Me

Full-Stack Developer focused on scalable systems, AI-driven products, and end-to-end execution.

I'm a full-stack developer who builds real-world, production-grade systems from responsive frontends to scalable backends and AI-powered workflows. I work across React/Next.js, FastAPI/Node.js, MongoDB/PostgreSQL, and cloud deployment stacks, delivering products that handle real-time updates, high concurrency, and large-scale data.

I’ve shipped complex platforms including real-time Kanban tools, AI interview simulators, document-processing engines for 100MB+ files, and multi-disease diagnostic systems powered by deep learning models. I enjoy taking ownership of projects: planning architecture, coordinating with teams, refining UX flows, and delivering clean, reliable systems.
