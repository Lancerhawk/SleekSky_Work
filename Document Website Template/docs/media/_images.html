Images

  1. Upload Options: Users can upload images with four distinct options, as illustrated in the interface, allowing for flexible integration into your content models. These options cater to various use cases, ensuring versatility in how images are added.
  2. View Modes: Images can be viewed in either a grid view or a list view, providing a customizable experience to suit different preferences and workflows.
  3. Transformation Capabilities: The tab enables users to adjust image properties such as width, height, and additional attributes, as demonstrated in the interface, offering precise control over visual presentation.
  4. Attribute Customization: Image attributes can be modified directly within the tab, allowing users to refine details such as alt text or alignment to optimize accessibility and layout.
  5. Supported Formats: Sleek CMS supports a wide range of image formats, including PNG, JPG, WebP, GIF, and more, ensuring compatibility with diverse media sources.
