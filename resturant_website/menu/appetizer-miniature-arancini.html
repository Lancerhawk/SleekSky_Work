Miniature Arancini

Crispy risotto bites with a molten mozzarella center.

Main Course
45 minutes
Prep Time
8 pieces
Serving
Medium
Spice Level
Available
Status
Price
$8.50

Description

These delightful arancini are crafted from creamy Arborio rice, infused with saffron and Parmesan, then rolled into small balls and fried to a perfect golden crisp. Each bite reveals a warm, gooey mozzarella core, making them an irresistible appetizer.

Ingredients

Arborio rice
1 cup

Chef's Notes

Ensure the risotto is completely cooled before forming the balls to prevent them from falling apart during frying. Serve immediately for the best texture.

Chef's Recommendation

A fantastic starter that pairs well with a light marinara dipping sauce.
